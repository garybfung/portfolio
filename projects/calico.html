Calico

Helping fashion brands track their production process

Creating products that people wear requires a persistant and systematic approach as a fashion product developer. If you're designing a hoodie, for example, you'll need to iterate on the product and plan the logistics, such as choosing the right drawstring, selecting a vendor to supply it, and ensuring timely delivery to the factory to put everything together. Often, these decisions do not go the way you want them to, impacting your go-to-market plans.

Many early-stage brands struggle to scale this process from one person to a small team; from one product to multiple product lines. Calico helps streamline this process for product teams and manufacturers, allowing you to see all of your material choices, such as jaw strings, leather, and fabrics, as well as tracking the production process across multiple products.

Overview

When I first joined Calico, they had just raised 2 million, announced later on Tech Crunch, and an initial mvp that some fashion companies were using to manage their products. I re-designed the product by building a design system and re-thinking its product flows to make the product more scalable and aligned with current industry knowledge.
