Calico

Improving how fashion brands track sourcing and production with Calico

Calico helps product teams and manufacturers streamline the manufacturing process. On a single platform, brands and manufacturers can collaborate to source materials, iterate on product specs and plan order logistics.

Overview

I joined Calico as their sole product designer, they had just raised 2 million, announced later on TechCrunch, and an initial mvp that some fashion companies were using to manage their products. I led product and design by building a design system and re-thinking its product flows to make the product more scalable and realistic to industry workflows.
