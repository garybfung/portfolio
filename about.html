About me

I'm a product designer based in Toronto driving growth by simplifying complex problems into functional solutions. I'm inspired by Reforge's growth loops and working in an empowered product team. I approach design by adapting my process depending on the nature of the problem, such as building product value from 0-1 or optimizing an existing flow to drive growth.

Previously, I worked at Calico during its seed stage, where I designed product management software to help fashion brands scale their manufacturing operations. Before that, I improved Hellohire's video career platform and built Uniqlo's design system.

Originally, I studied design at YSDN, York university and Sheridan College’s design program, where I learned about product and brand design.

Aside from design, I spend my winters snowboarding and maintaining Lakeridge’s Snowpark.
