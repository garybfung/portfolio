Devoted to designing in loops and building new value

Product designer who’s worked from early-stage startups to agencies. I tend to gravitate towards products that make people’s lives better.

Most recently, I worked at two pre-seed startups —Calico and Hellohire. I learned how difficult building a VC-backed startup can be as you have to manage product, growth and brand in order to gain traction. I put my knowledge to use on everything I’ve read and practiced—from understanding problem spaces, designing product loops, optimizing flows, learning industry knowledge and building for 0-1.

Prior to that, I worked on Uniqlo's Design System with Fast Retailing, Uniqlo's parent company, where I meticulously built components and learned about the design system governing models. The most exciting part was I got to work on a brand I loved and learned a lot about Japan’s work culture.

Before all this, I Graduated from York Sheridan's Bachelors of Design where I discovered brand and product design. I worked on brand and product projects with different new designers, that have grown so much. During this time, I was passionate about design systems, emerging technology and mobile apps.

Aside from design, you can find me shaping terrain park features at Lakeridge Ski Resort or playing Valorant.
