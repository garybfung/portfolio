About

I’m a product designer who grew up in Markham, a city close to Toronto in Canada. I’m passionate about building products that improve people’s lives.

I’m a firm believer that designers are people who are good at solving problems holistically and crafting intentful systems. Great products should easily blend into someone’s life and fit perfectly in place.

Most recently, I worked at Calico to build supply chain tools for fashion companies. Prior to that, I helped build Uniqlo, a design system, at Rangle and worked with Hellohire to build a product vision.
