Devoted to designing in loops and building new value

Product designer with experience from early stage startups to agencies. I'm drawn to products that improve people's lives.

Most recently, I worked at two pre-seed startups - Calico and Hellohire. My approach to design is rooted in understanding problem spaces, thinking in product loops and optimizing flows. I believe in the importance of designing for one and extending to many which often revolves around learning from industry experts and deeply learning about people.

Prior to this, I worked on the design system for Uniqlo, where I learned about building design foundations and how product teams work best. I also graduated from York Sheridan's design program, where I developed a passion for design systems, emerging technologies, and mobile apps.

Aside from design, you can find me snowboarding or shaping terrain park features at Lakeridge Ski Resort.
